Project Overview

MobTrack is an app that allows users to add their daily activities, and check their activity reports and suggestions from the information that is gathered from those activities. From that, they are able to keep track of their daily patterns, and reflect on the meaning that animates them.

Date: January 2021 - March 2021

Role: Product Manager, UX/UI Developer
