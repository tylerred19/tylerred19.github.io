What is RelayPlay?

RelayPlay is a platform that provides users with their top five personalized experiences each weekend created for them by local experts, as well as extra generalized experiences in the area.

Project Overview

Date: April 2021 to June 2021

Collaborators: Haeun (Madison) Kim, Vanessa (Nessi) Komar, Jessie Lin, and Lu Zhang

Focus: UX/UI Design, Visual Design

Tools: Figma

User Research

We created our own survey to send out but we were also able to see some feedback from previous user research that RelayPlay conducted. Thus, these are what we found out:

1. The users seemed to really appreciate the personalized experiences they were receiving

2. There was a lack of clarity at how to sign up for the events

3. Users aren’t able to save/look back at old experiences

4. Some users really liked the request button while others did not use the feature

5. Too much information about experience coordinator at top of screen

6. “How to navigate your weekend” had a chunk of useful information but users weren’t reading it

Problem Statement

How might we design a mobile/home page that’s more informative and user friendly?

User Personas

Amy Potter (21)

Wants/Needs

Wants to find places to have fun with her friends.

Can’t spend too much time on researching so she uses an app/website to search for experiences.

Frustrations

RelayPlay’s layout of experiences confuses her and is often hard to approach due to the website’s disorganization.

Nathan Greer (32)

Wants/Needs

Wishes to find new places to have fun in with his co workers and friends.

Wants to find events that fit his interests and likes. He wished he had recommendations from people who are knowledgeable with this area.

Frustrations

RelayPlay’s current “how to navigate” page is hard to find and the personalized page is confusing due to the expert’s profile page.
